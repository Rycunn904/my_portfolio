About

Hello! My name is Ryan, and I am a passionate self-taught developer with experience in web development, software engineering, and Roblox game development. I work with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, Lua, Java, and C#, while currently learning C++ and React to expand my skill set further. I enjoy building interactive websites, applications, bots, and custom systems inside Roblox Studio. One of my favorite parts of programming is solving problems and creating systems that are both functional and engaging for users. I have participated in multiple hackathons, including Virginia CyberSlam 2025 and 2026, where I collaborated with teams to solve technical and cybersecurity-related challenges. I also worked on a private AI and satellite imagery project that used image analysis and traffic data to help predict road locations from aerial imagery. Outside of coding, I continue improving my skills through personal projects, experimenting with new technologies, and learning more about software and game development every day.
